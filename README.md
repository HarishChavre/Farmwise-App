# Farmwise-App
Farmwise is a powerful smart agriculture dashboard built to help farmers optimize every aspect of their work. Combining real-time data, predictive analytics, and AI-driven tools, Farmwise simplifies decision-making on the farm.
🚀 Key Features:
🌤 Weather Forecasting: Hyper-local, real-time weather updates to help you plan with confidence.

🦠 Disease Detection: Get early alerts on potential crop diseases using AI and data analysis.

📈 Yield Prediction: Estimate crop yields based on environmental conditions and historical data.

💹 Market Prices: Stay ahead with real-time crop market prices and trends.

💬 Personal Chatbot Assistant: Ask your Farmwise Assistant anything—from “Will it rain tomorrow?” to “What’s the best time to harvest my rice?” Your smart farming companion is always ready to help.
